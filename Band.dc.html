Our Story

Born In North Texas, Built For The Stage

Texas, Whiskey & You came together for one reason — a shared obsession with the soul, grit, and songwriting of Chris Stapleton. What started as a handful of friends trading licks turned into one of North Texas’ most-requested tribute experiences.

We don’t do karaoke. Four seasoned musicians chase the real tone, the real dynamics, and that unmistakable gravel-and-soul vocal — then let the room take over.

Our mission is simple: give every crowd, in every kind of room, a night that feels like the real thing.
